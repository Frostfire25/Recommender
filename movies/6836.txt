Once again, Tiësto has left his mark on the history of dance music when he returns with a new installment of Tiësto In Concert. This time he has outdone himself by doing 2 shows in Arnhem, Holland (October 29th and 30th, 2004) and a show in Hasselt, Belgium (November 6th, 2004), sharing his music and vision with over 70,000 fans!
